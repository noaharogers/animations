Introduction

Our brain is wired in such a way that moving objects grab our attention. Because of this, adding animations and transitions is a great way to keep users engaged and highlight areas of importance within our projects.

Uses

Animations are powerful. We can add them to almost any element within our markup. Our animations can perform simple tasks like adjusting the background or text color when a user hovers over a link or more complex tasks like recreating the solar system with just CSS.

Walkthrough

Sure, recreating the solar system with CSS may not have pratical use within our projects, but there are a variety of animations that, if used correctly, can really add to the richness of our website.

Animation properties

The animation-direction defines whether or not the animation should play in normal, reverse or alternate cycles.

The animation-duration defines how long it should take the animation to complete one cycle.

The animation-iteration-count determines how many cycles the animation will play.
