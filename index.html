Introduction

Our brain is wired in such a way that moving objects grab our attention. Because of this, adding animations and transitions is a great way to keep users engaged and highlight areas of importance within our projects.

Uses

One great thing about animations, transforms and transitions is that they can be added to almost any element. We can can perform simple tasks like adjusting the background or text color when a user hovers over link or much more complex tasks like recreating the solar system with just CSS.

Walkthrough

Sure, recreating the solar system with CSS may not have everyday pratical use, but there are a variety of animations that, if used correctly, can really make your website stand out.

Let's go ahead and walkthrough the basics of an animation. Starting to use CSS animations is not very difficult and the requirements are fairly straightforward.
